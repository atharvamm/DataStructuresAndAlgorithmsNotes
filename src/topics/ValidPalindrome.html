Valid Palindrome

Approach

Again two approaches

1. Go full brute and first remove all elements that are not alphanum from the list, and then go back and check palindrome.

2. Other obvious method is two pointer where you check when you are at the location if its alphanum or not. And at the same time check palindrome while skipping non - alpha num. This way you can solve both while going through array only once.
