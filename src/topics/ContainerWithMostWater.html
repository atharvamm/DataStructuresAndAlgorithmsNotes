Container With Most Water

Approach

So the question essentially asks us to find the max area bw two bars.

To solve this we can look at all combinations and compare their areas and get max area.

Another method would be to use two pointers. This is the tricky part, the usual method is to use two pointers from start, where one pointer follows another. Here we can see that it is helpful for the pointers to start from ends.
